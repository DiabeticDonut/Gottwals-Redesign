Community Involvement

At Gottwals and Walls of Books®, we maintain partnerships with civic groups, schools, churches, and charities.

To keep abreast of school reading needs, we make sure local families know Gottwals Books and Walls of Books® will have their school books in stock. We also do promotions that include giveaways, discounts, and all kinds of family fun. Gottwals Books and Walls of Books® host book clubs, kids book readings, and author events. We are always coming up with fresh, new ideas to keep the customers flocking through the doors. On top of all this, we donate thousands of books to local groups each and every year!
